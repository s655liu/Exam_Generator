Support & Documentation

🚀 Getting Started

Select your subject and course code. Our AI will automatically fetch the relevant topics from the UW course calendar. Select the topics you want to prioritize and click generate.

📊 Math & Graphs

We use KaTeX for all mathematical notations. If you're in a STAT or AMATH course, the AI may also generate interactive Chart.js visualizations for probability distributions.

🖨️ Exporting Exams

Once generated, use the 'Print to PDF' button to save a high-quality, formatted version of your exam and answer key.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

If generation fails, try selecting fewer topics or refreshing the page. Ensure your network allows connections to our generation API.
